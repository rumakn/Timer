About

What is it?

The Pomodoro Technique is a system of working where you break down your time into smaller sections to help manage productivity. You are more productive when you are given a chance to take a break every so often and can break down tasks into smaller chunks of work time. This technique allows you to schedule working time and break times.

How can it be customized?

With this pomodoro timer you can add two custom timers using the + buttons on the top of the timer or in the settings. You can also delete and edit the timers through the settings section to make the timer work however you would like. Each timer can also have its own alert sound. You can also edit the loop so that you create your own schedule that works best for you.
